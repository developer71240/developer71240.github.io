Crop to Opaque - A Photoshop-extending Script

This script for use with Adobe Photoshop® & Adobe Photoshop Elements® will trim the edges of an image to remove all transparency. Practical uses are trimming after: merging photos into a panorama, transforming, warping, or rotating.

Tip: undoing the last two history states in photoshop after running this script will result in the opaque area being selected but not trimmed.
